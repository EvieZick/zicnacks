our knickknacks

Knickknacks can be all sorts of things, but I've found I specifically enjoy crocheting above all else. It's a slow but rewarding process, and it keeps my hands and brain busy when I'm doing something else like watching TV or hanging out with friends.
I started crocheting making things like coasters, which then evolved into making little mushrooms and other trinkets. I'm working on expanding my collection, so keep your eyes peeled for more to come.
Some knickknacks include some unique thrift finds and prints of Evie's artworks. We hope you enjoy these fun little trinkets, because we sure do.
-Bode Anderson-Brown
