our earrings

Once I became more familiar with making jewelry, I decided to take up making earrings. Earrings are so fun because they open a new world of possibilities, but also require a better understanding of how to use tools to create the shapes you want.
For me, learning how to make earrings was a bit more frustrating then necklaces. I had a hard time figuring out how to bend metal and connect pieces without damaging the bead. However, once I got comfortable with the process, it's become my favorite form of jewelry to make.
Typically making earrings takes less time than beading a necklace, which has allowed me to expand my creativity with earring making at a rapid rate. You'll need some tools, but making earrings is well worth the investment.
-Evie Zick
