our necklaces

Necklaces are what started off ZICNACS. Before I ventured into more complicated mediums of jewelry that require tools and technical skill, I was putting bead after bead on a wire and finishing it off with a simple crimp and clasp.
What I found so therapeutic about making necklaces is that it can easily be a mindless task. Some of my favorite works have started out with no plans--just placing bead after bead and seeing where it takes you. For those looking to get into beading, necklaces are a great start. There are some great resources to teach beginners where to get started.
-Evie Zick
